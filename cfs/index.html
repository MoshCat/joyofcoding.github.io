Joy of Coding: call for sessions

Sessions can be in the form of a workshop, tutorial, talk, discussion, hands-on coding session or other form of 'un-session'. Please mind the following criteria for your session. We are looking forward to your submission!

  • Encourage participation: we prefer sessions that enable attendees to actively participate, through hands-on coding, discussion or other inspiring ways of interaction.
  • Introduce novelty: topics should pertain to new and emerging technologies, introducing new and improved ways of dealing with existing challenges or dealing with entirely new challenges altogether.
  • Be technology-agnostic: a workshop preferably imposes little or no barriers to entry for potential participants, i.e. participants can participate choosing a prefered technology stack from a number of options.
  • Time-boxed execution: the session length will be either 1 hour (presentation) or 2 hours(hands-on workshop).

Submit your session

All submissions will be evaluated and selected based on the above mentioned session requirements and the relation of the proposal to the overall conference theme and programme. This call closes on the 7th of December 2013.
