Come fly with me

What can be more Joyful then flying a robot with you computer? Flying a room full of robots!

Come and join the fun in this exciting side track where you get the chance to fly a parrot ardrone. We will provide the drones, you must provide the brains.

In this side track we have setup various drones and virtual machines that allow you to program the drones in various languages. After a short safety instruction you will be able to control the drones from your laptop.
