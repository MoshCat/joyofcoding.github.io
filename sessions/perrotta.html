Java is from Mars, Ruby is from Venus

For Java programmers is difficult to think in Ruby. For Ruby programmers is difficult to think in Java. In this talk Paolo will show us these two different kind of language philosophies and why are not easily interchangeable

Paolo Perrotta

Paolo "Nusco" Perrotta has more than ten years of experience as a developer and writer. He worked for domains ranging from embedded to enterprise software, computer games, and web applications. He describes himself as an "Agile Coach, rubyist, Generic Italian Guy, author of Metaprogramming Ruby".
