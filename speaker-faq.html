Hi awesome Joy of Coding speaker! Thanks for making Joy of Coding great with us. On this page you find some bits of information you might need before or during Joy of Coding.

What connectors will be available for the presentation?

The venue told us that the main room (where the keynotes will take place), named Willem Burger Zaal, has HDMI/DVI connectors. The beamers in the other rooms have VGA connectors. It is probably a good idea to bring any convertor cables you have (we will do aswell), just in case.

What is the aspect ratio of the beamer used for my presentation?

All beamers are 16:9.

Will my presentation be recorded, and put online?

Possibly. All presentations in the main room (where the keynotes take place), named Willem Burger Zaal, will be recorded and put online by InfoQ. Besides that we will have someone create an "after movie" of the event, whom might also record some minutes of your talk/workshop and may even ask you some questions.

How much time do I have for my talk?

The time slots for talks are all 30 minutes, including Q&A. We really hope you leave some time for Q&A, but it is up to you. We will make sure all talk finish on time as attendees need time to find their next talk.

Where is the venue?

We created a page for that. The venue consists of several areas, each with their own entrance. Our area is call Willem Burger for which you can see the entrance on Google Street View by following this link.

Where is the hotel?

All the rooms we have arranged are in the Manhattan (soon to be Marriot) hotel. By following the link in the previous sentence you should be able to find details of the hotel.

Help, there are problems!

The emergency number in the Netherlands is 112. In case of minor emergencies, you can reach out to the organisers of the event: Martin (+31.681.488.635), Cies (+31.646.469.087) and Felienne (+31.647.216.980).

Will there be dinner on Friday?

Yes, the event includes all foods developers love: pizza, hot dogs and noodles, which will be served after the last keynote at the venue.
