Hulstkamp Gebouw

Rotterdam knows the Hulstkamp Gebouw (1892) from their grandfather's stories as a proud landmark on the Noordereiland, which lies in the river Maas. Authentic and grand from the outside, contemporary and stylish inside. The building, one of the 518 national monuments of the city, has survived the history of Rotterdam.
